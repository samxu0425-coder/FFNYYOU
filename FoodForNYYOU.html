The Problem

Hidden hunger meets unnecessary waste — every single day on campus.

1 in 3
college students in NYC
experience food insecurity

Students at NYU face hunger daily, yet nothing is being done about it. Meanwhile, NYU clubs and dining halls produce substantial food waste…

Food Insecurity is Hidden

Many students don't disclose hunger due to stigma, and there's no reliable system to identify or reach them.

Food Waste at Scale

Club events and dining halls regularly discard surplus food with no redistribution pathway.

No Unified Solution

Existing resources like Violet Pantry and ShareMeals are fragmented, limited in scope, or no longer campus-specific.

Our Solution

Turning campus surplus into student support.

OUR MISSION

Reduce food insecurity at NYU through efficient food redistribution.

We connect clubs that have surplus food with students who need it — turning waste into impact through a simple, volunteer-powered platform.

OUR VISION

A campus with no food waste and no student hunger.

We envision an NYU where every event leftover feeds someone in need — where surplus is seen as a resource, not refuse.

How It Works

Three simple steps. No forms. No payment. Just food.

1

Collect

Club finishes their event → volunteers pick up and properly package the leftover food.

2

Redistribute

Volunteers transport packaged food to centralized pickup locations around campus.

3

Access

Students pick up food during allotted pickup windows. No forms. No payment.

Our Social Impact Goals

What we're working toward, every semester.

01

Impact Food Insecurity

Make a meaningful, measurable impact on food insecurity at NYU by connecting hungry students with free, nutritious food that would otherwise go to waste.

02

Reduce Food Waste

Reduce food waste at NYU, particularly from club events, transforming leftovers into a resource rather than refuse.

03

Spread Awareness & Inspire

Raise campus-wide awareness of food insecurity, and inspire other student organizations and institutions to launch similar initiatives.
